Property Inspection Checklist

Room-by-room inspection form for landlords. Works for move-in, move-out, and annual inspections. Built from the same template that runs inside LANDLORDR ($89/mo standalone or in the PF9 Property Suite at $119/mo).

What's on the checklist

  • • Header — property address, unit, inspector, date, inspection type
  • • Exterior — roof, siding, gutters, walkways, landscaping
  • • Interior (per room) — walls, floors, ceilings, windows, doors
  • • Kitchen — appliances, countertops, plumbing, ventilation
  • • Bathrooms — fixtures, plumbing, ventilation, sealant
  • • Systems — HVAC, water heater, electrical panel, smoke/CO detectors
  • • Safety — locks, egress, fire extinguisher, GFCI outlets
  • • Photo references — numbered photo log
  • • Sign-off — landlord + tenant signature blocks

Why we made this

Most landlords with 5–50 units inspect with a clipboard, a phone camera, and a list they wrote in 2019. That's fine until a security-deposit dispute lands in small-claims court and you need to prove the dent in the wall was there before move-in.

A consistent inspection form per property — same fields every time, tenant-signed, photos numbered — is the cheapest insurance you can buy against deposit disputes. This template gives you that structure. Print it, photocopy it, fill it out.
