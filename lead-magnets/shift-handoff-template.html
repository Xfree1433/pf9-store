Shift Handoff Template

One-page printable sheet supervisors fill out at end of shift so the next shift's lead walks in already knowing what happened. Production summary, open issues, incidents, and next-shift tasks. Built from the same template that runs inside SHIFTLOG ($49/mo).

What's on the sheet

  • • Header — date, shift, supervisor, line/area
  • • Production summary — units run, scrap, downtime causes
  • • Open issues — equipment, materials, people
  • • Incidents — what happened, severity, action taken
  • • Next-shift tasks — priority list for the incoming team
  • • Notes — free-form for anything else
  • • Sign-off — outgoing and incoming supervisor

Why we made this

We built PF9 because most manufacturing software is overpriced and over-complicated. The shift handoff problem is real — most shops we've talked to still hand off shifts on a clipboard or a whiteboard. That's fine when the shop is small. It breaks when you scale to 3 shifts and 5 lines.

This template is the same structure that runs inside SHIFTLOG, our shift-management app ($49/mo flat, unlimited users). Print it out, photocopy it, use it on a clipboard until paper starts to hurt — then graduate to the app when you're ready.

It's free. We're not gating it for "qualified lead" reasons. We're collecting emails so we can tell you when we ship more templates (NCR/CAPA log, inspection checklists, work-order templates) and the occasional product update.
