How we calculated this

Last verified against vendor pricing pages April 2026. Vendors increasingly move to quote-based pricing — numbers below are approximations of entry-tier cost.

PF9 Property Suite — $119/mo flat

LANDLORDR + TENANTLINK bundle. No per-unit fee, no per-user fee. Price does not change from 1 unit to 500. Published on our property page.

Buildium Essential — from $62/mo

Buildium's pricing page lists Essential "starting at $62/month" but does not publicly disclose per-unit fees or thresholds. Our estimate assumes the first ~30 units are covered by the base fee and each additional unit adds roughly $1.50/mo — the commonly reported rate from public Buildium quotes. For an exact number, request a quote directly.

AppFolio Core — 50-unit minimum

AppFolio's pricing page states: "Minimum spend and 50 unit minimum apply." Below 50 units, AppFolio is not an option. Above 50 units, exact pricing requires a quote; our estimate uses the commonly reported $1.49/unit with a $298/mo floor.

DoorLoop Starter — $69/mo, max 10 units

DoorLoop's Starter plan is capped at 10 units. Above 10 units, the Pro plan is $189/mo (monthly billing) or $149/mo (annual billing). Above 300 units, DoorLoop requires a custom quote. Our calculator uses monthly-billing prices.

This comparison uses publicly listed entry-tier pricing. Real quotes depend on required features (accounting, marketing, maintenance, 1099s, ACH), contract length, annual-vs-monthly billing, and region. Always get a direct quote from each vendor before deciding. Per-unit fees often stack with add-ons for electronic payments, screening, and document storage that are included in PF9.
