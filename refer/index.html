Terms (the short version)

Commission

20% of the customer's monthly subscription revenue, paid every month they remain active, for up to 12 months from the date of their first paid subscription.

Attribution

Anyone who subscribes within 90 days of clicking your referral link or using your referral code is attributed to you. First-touch attribution — if a prospect uses multiple codes, the earliest wins.

Refunds

If a referred customer takes the 14-day money-back guarantee, no commission is paid. After the guarantee window, partial-month refunds are at our discretion but commissions for months already paid are not clawed back.

Payouts

Quarterly via ACH (US-based affiliates) or PayPal (international). $50 minimum payout — anything below rolls over to the next quarter.

Disqualifying behavior

Brand-bidding ads (e.g., Google Ads on "plainspokenfoundrynine"), spam, cold-bulk-email campaigns, coupon scraping, fake accounts, or any promotion that misrepresents PF9. We disqualify and reclaim unpaid commissions if any of these are found.

Termination

Either party can end the relationship in writing with 30 days notice. We continue to pay out earned commissions for active referrals through the 12-month window regardless of termination, unless termination was for disqualifying behavior.

Tax

You're an independent contractor, responsible for your own taxes. US-based affiliates earning over $600/year get a 1099-NEC.

Common questions

How much do affiliates earn?

20% of the customer's monthly subscription, every month, for 12 months. A Manufacturing Suite referral pays $49.80/mo × 12 = $597.60. A Property Suite referral pays $23.80/mo × 12 = $285.60.

When and how do I get paid?

Quarterly via ACH or PayPal. $50 minimum payout. Each payment includes attribution detail.

What does the referral link look like?

Plain English code. e.g., store.plainspokenfoundrynine.com/?ref=your-name. Easy to remember, easy to mention on a podcast.

Can I refer myself or my own company?

No. Self-referrals are disqualifying. If your company subscribes through you, that's just a customer — not a referral.

Can I negotiate higher than 20%?

Not at this stage. The 20% is uniform across all partners. We may revisit for partners driving significant volume after 12 months.

Is there a leaderboard or contest?

No. We don't gamify referrals. The commission is the commission. We may publish a "thanks to our partners" page eventually if affiliates want public credit.
