# PF9 Free Directory Submission Playbook

**Goal:** get PF9 listed on 15+ free SaaS / startup / software directories. Each listing yields (a) a permanent backlink to the storefront, which helps organic SEO, and (b) a small amount of long-tail discovery traffic from users browsing the directory.

**Time investment:** ~5 minutes per submission × 15 directories = 75 minutes spread across a week.
**Expected impact:** 1–3 prospects per month per directory at maturity, plus measurable organic SEO lift from authoritative backlinks.

## Tier 1 — High-authority free directories (do these first)

These are the highest-leverage submissions. ~30 min total.

### 1. AlternativeTo
- **URL:** https://alternativeto.net/ — top-right "Add a software"
- **Need:** Software name, category, description, logo, screenshot. Optional: link existing alternatives.
- **PF9-specific strategy:** submit as an alternative to Buildium, AppFolio, DoorLoop, MRPeasy, Katana, Fishbowl (you can list multiple alternatives per submission). Drives the most direct buyer-intent traffic of any directory.
- **Review time:** 1–7 days for moderator approval
- **Backlink quality:** High (DA 80+)

### 2. SaaSHub
- **URL:** https://www.saashub.com/contact — request listing via contact form
- **Need:** Same fields as AlternativeTo, plus link to public pricing
- **PF9-specific strategy:** mention you want listings under "MES alternatives" and "Property Management Software"
- **Review time:** 1–2 weeks
- **Backlink quality:** Medium-high (DA 60+)

### 3. Crunchbase (free profile)
- **URL:** https://www.crunchbase.com/ — sign up for free account, then "Add a company"
- **Need:** Founding date, founders, headquarters, brief description, logo, website
- **PF9-specific strategy:** mark as "private", "bootstrapped", no funding rounds. Tag verticals: SaaS, Manufacturing, Property Tech
- **Review time:** 3–5 business days
- **Backlink quality:** Very high (DA 90+) — even if low-traffic, the backlink alone is worth it

### 4. Product Hunt (re-submission for the second vertical)
- **URL:** https://www.producthunt.com/posts/new
- **Status:** You may have already done a manufacturing launch (covered in `MARKETING.md`). If only one vertical is launched, do the other one separately.
- **Strategy:** never launch both verticals on the same day — split them. Each one is a fresh PH launch.
- **Backlink quality:** Very high (DA 80+) plus a possible spike of launch-day traffic

### 5. BetaList
- **URL:** https://betalist.com/submit
- **Need:** Tagline, description, launch URL, optional invite code
- **PF9-specific strategy:** angle the submission as "the no-per-seat alternative to {Buildium, MRPeasy}" rather than "a new SaaS"
- **Review time:** 1–3 weeks (they curate)
- **Backlink quality:** Medium-high (DA 60+) plus a niche audience of early-adopter startups
- **Note:** if rejected for not being "early stage enough," skip and move to others

## Tier 2 — General SaaS directories (~30 min total)

### 6. Indie Hackers Products
- **URL:** https://www.indiehackers.com/products → "Add product"
- **Need:** product name, tagline, URL, founders, optional revenue. **You don't have to disclose revenue.**
- **PF9-specific:** join the IH community first (sign up free), then submit product. Engage in the community for 1 week to avoid looking spammy.
- **Backlink quality:** High (DA 80+) plus a maker-founder audience

### 7. Slant
- **URL:** https://www.slant.co/ — search "manufacturing software" or similar, then "Add option" if PF9 isn't listed
- **Format:** community-voted comparison lists (e.g., "What are the best property management apps?")
- **PF9-specific:** add PF9 as an option under multiple existing lists; provide pros + cons
- **Backlink quality:** Medium (DA 60+)

### 8. Software Suggest (free listing tier)
- **URL:** https://www.softwaresuggest.com/get-listed
- **Need:** Full product info, screenshots, pricing, description
- **Note:** they'll try to upsell you on paid plans — decline and stick with the free listing
- **Backlink quality:** Medium (DA 60+)

### 9. SaaSworthy
- **URL:** https://www.saasworthy.com/contact-us
- **Format:** request a free listing via contact form
- **PF9-specific:** mention which vertical categories you want listed under
- **Review time:** 1–3 weeks
- **Backlink quality:** Medium (DA 50+)

### 10. F6S
- **URL:** https://www.f6s.com/ → sign up as a startup
- **Free profile** + accelerator / program directory
- **Backlink quality:** Medium (DA 70+) — mostly for founder visibility, but the backlink helps

### 11. Stack Share

These take longer to set up because they involve a real integration, but they pay off forever.

## Tactical notes

1. **Submit slowly, not all at once.** 2–3 per day spread over a week looks organic. 15 in one day can flag as low-effort.
2. **Customize the description per directory.** Don't paste the same 500-character description into every form. AlternativeTo wants "what is this an alternative to" framing; SaaSHub wants categories; BetaList wants the "new and interesting" angle.
3. **Use a forwarding email or alias** in submission fields if you want to track which directory generated which support inquiries.
4. **Decline upsells.** Many directories will email you within a week asking if you want a paid "featured" placement for $50–$500/mo. The free listing alone is usually enough to start. Re-evaluate paid only if a specific directory drives meaningful traffic.
5. **Update listings when prices change.** If you ever change pricing, update the directories — outdated pricing on third-party sites confuses prospects.
