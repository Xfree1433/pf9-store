Questions manufacturers ask first

Is there really no per-user or per-seat fee?

Correct. $249/mo for the Manufacturing Suite covers unlimited supervisors, unlimited inspectors, unlimited operators, and unlimited office users. The price does not change whether you have 20 employees or 250.

How does this compare to enterprise MES or ERP pricing?

Enterprise MES and ERP platforms typically quote $500+/user/month plus a 3–6 month implementation and $30k–$80k in year-one services. Mid-market options like Katana, MRPeasy, and Fishbowl charge per-user fees that scale with headcount. PF9 Manufacturing Suite is $249/mo flat at any team size, deploys in days, and exports cleanly to QuickBooks and CSV.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes — every PF9 subscription comes with a 14-day money-back guarantee. Subscribe, load real shift data and inventory, and if it's not the right fit, email support@plainspokenfoundrynine.com within 14 days for a full refund. Prefer to see it first? Watch all 5 demos above.

What kind of manufacturer is this designed for?

Discrete and light-process manufacturers with 20–250 employees. Job shops, contract manufacturers, fabrication shops, assembly operations, food packaging, and similar. Not a good fit for tier-1 automotive, regulated pharma, or single-line consumer goods at scale.

Do the apps integrate with QuickBooks or our existing ERP?

All five apps export to CSV today, and FLOWTRACK and REPORTR export directly to QuickBooks Online format. Native API integrations for ERP vendors are on the roadmap. If you already have an ERP and need real-time bidirectional sync, we are not the right fit yet.

What happens if I cancel?

Cancel any time from your account. Your data exports to CSV and PDF before cancellation. No penalties, no contracts.
