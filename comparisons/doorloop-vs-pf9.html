DoorLoop vs PF9 Property Suite

DoorLoop is a polished modern alternative to legacy property management software. PF9 takes a different angle — flat pricing, no tier breaks, no annual contract. Here's where each fits.

Choose DoorLoop if
  • • You're at exactly 10 or fewer units (Starter $69/mo is genuinely cheap)
  • • Built-in payments processing matters more than flat pricing
  • • You want the polished mobile app DoorLoop is known for
  • • Annual billing for a discount is something you want
Choose PF9 if
  • • You manage 11+ units (DoorLoop bumps you to $189/mo)
  • • You don't want annual contracts or tier breaks
  • • You want unlimited team members included
  • • You'd rather connect your own payment processor than use a built-in one

What DoorLoop does well

Honest sections aren't a sales tactic — they're how you make a real decision.

Polished modern UI

DoorLoop's interface is the most polished in the property management space. Onboarding flows are tight, the mobile app is well-built, and the product genuinely feels modern. PF9 is intentionally simpler — fewer features, faster screens, less surface to learn.

Built-in payments processing

DoorLoop has its own ACH and card processing built into the product. Tenants pay through DoorLoop's portal; landlords get the funds with reconciled records. PF9 doesn't process payments; you'd connect your existing processor (Stripe, Plaid, ACH provider) and TENANTLINK collects the receipt.

Annual billing discount

DoorLoop Pro is $189/mo on monthly billing or $149/mo on annual. If you're committed to a year, that discount is real. PF9 doesn't offer annual discounts — same flat $119/mo whether you pay monthly or quit next month.

Where PF9 wins

No 10-unit cliff

DoorLoop's Starter caps at 10 units. The day you sign your 11th lease, your bill jumps from $69 to $189/mo. PF9 stays at $119/mo whether you have 5 units or 250.

No annual contract

DoorLoop's discount tiers reward annual commitment. PF9's flat $119/mo applies on month-to-month billing. Cancel any time, no early-termination clauses.

Unlimited team members included

Property managers, leasing agents, accountants, and contractors all get accounts at no extra cost. DoorLoop has user limits in the lower tiers.

14-day money-back guarantee

Subscribe, run the business through PF9, get a full refund if it's not the fit. DoorLoop's refund policy depends on whether you're on monthly or annual billing.

Migrating from DoorLoop to PF9

  1. Export from DoorLoop: Settings → Data Export → CSV for properties, units, leases, tenants, and rent ledger.
  2. Import to LANDLORDR: setup wizard accepts the CSV format directly.
  3. Reconcile rent ledger: spot-check tenant balances against DoorLoop.
  4. Switch payment processing: if you used DoorLoop's built-in payments, you'll need to set up a separate processor (Stripe, ACH provider, etc.) for TENANTLINK to reference.
  5. Notify tenants: TENANTLINK gives each tenant a new portal login.
  6. Cancel DoorLoop at end of billing period.

If you're on annual billing with DoorLoop, check your contract before canceling. Email support@plainspokenfoundrynine.com for migration help.
