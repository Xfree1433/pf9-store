How we write these

Every comparison page on this site is structured the same way: a quick-verdict box, a real pricing comparison at multiple team or portfolio sizes, an honest "what they do well" section, a "where PF9 wins" section, a migration walkthrough, and an FAQ.

The "what they do well" section is not a sales tactic. We name real strengths — integrations, mature accounting, polished UI, ecosystem depth — that the competitor genuinely has and PF9 doesn't. The point is to help you make a real decision, not to win at any cost.

Pricing comes from publicly available vendor pages and quoted figures from comparison sites, dated to May 2026. Real quotes vary based on contract terms, modules, and region — always verify on the vendor's own pricing page.
