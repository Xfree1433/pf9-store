AppFolio vs PF9 Property Suite

AppFolio is built for mid-market and enterprise property managers — and it requires a 50-unit minimum. PF9 is built for the 5–50 unit segment AppFolio won't sell to. Here's where each one fits.

Choose AppFolio if
  • • You manage 100+ units across multiple property types
  • • You need built-in marketing syndication to Zillow, Trulia, etc.
  • • Integrated AI leasing and tenant screening matter
  • • You're comfortable with a quote-based contract
Choose PF9 if
  • • You have under 50 units (AppFolio won't sell to you)
  • • You want published pricing without a sales call
  • • You'd rather grow without your software bill growing
  • • You want to deploy in an afternoon, not a quarter

What AppFolio does well

Honest sections aren't a sales tactic — they're how you make a real decision.

Listing syndication built in

AppFolio pushes vacancies to Zillow, Trulia, Apartments.com, and dozens of other listing sites in one click. Lead-to-application workflow is integrated. PF9 doesn't have native syndication; you'd post listings separately.

AI leasing assistant and tenant screening

AppFolio's AI handles leasing inquiries, schedules showings, and runs screening through a single workflow. For property managers handling high lead volume, this is genuinely valuable. PF9 doesn't have AI leasing or screening today.

Mid-market accounting and reporting

AppFolio runs full accrual accounting with bank-rec, owner statements, and 1099 generation built in. If you're managing on behalf of multiple owners with complex draw structures, AppFolio's accounting is a real differentiator. PF9 exports to QuickBooks for that workflow.

Where PF9 wins

Available below 50 units

AppFolio won't sell to you under 50 units. PF9 will. If you have 5, 10, or 30 units, AppFolio is not an option you can choose. PF9 was built specifically for that segment.

Published pricing

$119/mo flat. No quote, no sales call, no minimum spend. AppFolio requires a quote conversation; PF9 lets you subscribe in 60 seconds.

Flat at any unit count

The price doesn't change as you grow. AppFolio's $1.49/unit fee scales linearly above the floor. At 250 units, AppFolio is roughly $370/mo — PF9 is still $119/mo.

No implementation

AppFolio onboarding is typically a multi-week process. PF9 deploys in an afternoon — subscribe, import your CSVs, run the business.

Migrating from AppFolio to PF9

If you decide to switch:

  1. Export from AppFolio: properties, units, leases, tenants, and rent ledger as CSV. Available under Settings → Data.
  2. Import to LANDLORDR: setup wizard handles the CSV format directly.
  3. Verify rent ledger: spot-check a few tenants' balances against AppFolio.
  4. Notify tenants: TENANTLINK gives each tenant a new portal login.
  5. Cancel AppFolio at month-end after confirming everything's running.

Note: AppFolio's annual contract may have early-termination implications. Read your contract before canceling. Email support@plainspokenfoundrynine.com for migration help.
