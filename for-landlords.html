Questions landlords ask first

Is there really no per-unit or per-door fee?

Correct. $119/mo covers unlimited units, unlimited tenants, and unlimited team members. The price does not change whether you manage 5 units or 500.

How does this compare to Buildium or AppFolio?

Buildium and AppFolio charge monthly per unit (typically $1.50–$2.00/unit with a $50–$300 minimum). At 20 units, that's roughly $250–$300/mo. PF9 Property Suite is $119/mo flat at any unit count.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes. Every app has a live demo with sample data pre-loaded. No signup, no credit card required.

Do you handle ACH rent collection and 1099s?

ACH rent collection is on the roadmap for Q3 2026. Today LANDLORDR tracks rent receipts, generates ledgers, and exports to QuickBooks and CSV for your existing payment processor and tax workflow.

What happens if I cancel?

Cancel any time from your account. Your data exports to CSV and PDF before cancellation. No penalties, no contracts.
