## 2. Engagement Summary

Cowork will run demand generation and sales operations for the PF9 App Store (https://store.plainspokenfoundrynine.com) across the four product verticals defined in `SALES_PLAN.md` (manufacturing, AI ops, property management, compliance). The goal is to convert PF9 from launch-complete to repeatable monthly revenue within the 90-day term.

`SALES_PLAN.md`, as it exists at the Effective Date, is incorporated by reference and governs execution detail (ICPs, channel mix, messaging guardrails, KPI targets). If this SOW and `SALES_PLAN.md` conflict, this SOW controls.

## 3. Scope of Services

### 3.1 In scope
1. **Paid media** — Google Search and LinkedIn Ads setup, management, and optimization within the approved budget
2. **Outbound sales** — list building (3 segments × 500 contacts), multi-touch email + LinkedIn sequencing, SDR activity
3. **Content** — two blog posts per week, one case study every six weeks, repurposing of existing `MARKETING.md` assets into a 4-week social drip
4. **Conversion optimization** — copy and layout A/B tests on the existing storefront (no re-platforming)
5. **Lifecycle email** — trial → paid, paid → expand, churn-save sequences
6. **Sales operations** — CRM setup and hygiene, demo scheduling, pipeline management, demo-led close assist for the first 20 bundle deals
7. **Reporting** — weekly one-page pipeline + revenue report; final readout deck in week 12

### 3.2 Out of scope
- Product or feature development
- Storefront re-platforming or visual redesign
- Pricing changes, new SKUs, or discount programs below list
- Trademark, PR, or brand-identity work
- Any campaign that misrepresents flat-rate pricing or "no per-user fees" claims
- Enterprise (1,000+ employee) targeting

Out-of-scope work requires a signed Change Order (§9).
